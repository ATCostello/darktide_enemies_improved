# Enemies Improved (Healthbars - Debuffs - Outlines and more)

https://www.nexusmods.com/warhammer40kdarktide/mods/809

I have been steadily working on Enemies Improved for a while now, and after an early-access period in the Darktide Modding Discord - It is finally time to release my next mod to the world on Nexusmods!

Enemies Improved is a combination of a bunch of brand-new (Made from scratch) features that focus on *improving* enemies within Darktide. Right now, this includes healthbars, debuff tracking, special attack warnings, outlines and above-head markers. A more in-depth overview of all of these features can be found in the sections below. 

Enemies Improved was built from the ground-up with customisation in mind. What you see in the screenshots and by default is purely how I have customised the UI for my current tastes. Many features, layouts and colours can be customised via the in-game mod settings menu. Further down the page you will find a more in-depth look at each setting and what you can adjust.

I have made Enemies improved to be as optimised, smooth and bug-free as possible, building on features as time went on. This means you should get a smooth experience even in scenes with many enemies all at once. I have collected my findings and knowledge of Darktide modding from the past few years into every nook and cranny of this mod, consider it my pièce de résistance of my Darktide modding journey. 

Also, yes. I am fully aware of other mods on Nexus that on the surface level may seem to achieve similar results. Just know that Enemies Improved was built by my hands with over 100 hours of writing code, testing and iterating over a period of several months. I built this to achieve a cohesive healthbar/debuff/warning system that was optimised and customisable to fit my needs, and hopefully you can find enjoyment from this too!

Every 'module' can be completely disabled from the mod settings menu too, so if you only want the debuff tracking, or just the special attack warnings - you can decide!

Please take a look at the rest of the page for a deeper-dive into Enemies Improved, and be sure to check out the FAQ/Compatibility section at the bottom.
